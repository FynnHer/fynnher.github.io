Welcome to My Blog

By Fynn-Lasse Herrmann

I'm Fynn-Lasse Herrmann, an 18-year-old developer and student from Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. After working on various software projects over the past few years, I've finally decided to create a space where I can share my thoughts, experiences, and learnings with the world.

What to Expect

This blog will cover a variety of topics that I'm passionate about:

  • Python & AI - Tutorials, project showcases, and insights into machine learning and image analysis
  • Mobile Development - My experiences building cross-platform apps with Flutter
  • Systems Programming - My journey learning Rust and exploring kernel development
  • DLRG Projects - Software I've built for the German Life Saving Association
  • General Coding - Tips, tricks, and thoughts on software development

Why I Started This Blog

As I prepare for my dual computer science degree next year, I realized how much I've learned over the years that could be helpful to others. Whether you're just starting your coding journey or you're a fellow developer looking for new perspectives, I hope you'll find something valuable here.

Writing also helps me solidify my own understanding of topics. As they say, the best way to learn is to teach!

A Bit About My Journey

I started programming a few years ago, and since then, I've worked on projects ranging from AI-powered image analysis tools to mobile applications. Currently, I have a Flutter app in beta testing, and I'm excited to see it grow.

Outside of personal projects, I'm an active member of the DLRG (Deutsche Lebens-Rettungs-Gesellschaft), where I contribute both as a volunteer and as a developer. Building software that helps save lives is incredibly rewarding.
